Run a plant shop. Grow things. Make money. Spend money on growing more things faster.

You move left and right behind the counter, tending your plants and serving the stream of customers who come in wanting specific ones. A fully-grown plant sells for double its normal value when a customer is waiting for it. Every dollar goes back into expanding your store, buying new plant types, or upgrading your legs.

<b>Plants</b> grow in three stages. Water them when they're ready (they'll let you know) and they'll advance to the next stage. Fully grown plants can be sold at the counter or cloned with the Grafter to fill your shelves faster.

<b>Customers</b> are a mixed crowd. Most just want whatever you're growing. But a handful keep coming back — a monocled investor, a mayoral candidate on her fifth campaign rebrand, a spy who needs cactus thorns for close-quarters combat. Each has a few chapters to their story. Serving them unlocks the next visit.

<b>Upgrades</b> are purchased from the PC in the corner:
<ul>
<li>New plant types (Cactus, Rose, Tulip, Daisy, Golden Lotus)</li>
<li>Extra store slots</li>
<li>Speed boosts (three tiers; each one changes your character color)</li>
<li>Watering Can</li>
<li>Grafter (clones fully-grown plants)</li>
<li>Water Drone (waters plants automatically)</li>
</ul>
